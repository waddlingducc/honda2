Overview: Honda Civic Si

The sport compact for the rest of us.

The Civic Si has been the affordable sport compact benchmark for nearly two decades, and the 2026 model continues that tradition. A turbocharged 1.5L produces 200 horsepower routed through a precise six-speed manual transmission and a helical limited-slip differential. Rev-match downshifting, adaptive dampers on the high-spec Si, and standard Honda Sensing make it as livable as it is fun. The Si is the sweet spot in the Civic range for driving enthusiasts who can't quite swing the Type R.

How It Compares to Competitors

The Civic Si's only true rival is the VW GTI, which costs $4,000 more and is only available with an automatic transmission. The Hyundai Elantra N is faster but considerably more expensive. For under $31k, the Si remains the affordable enthusiast choice.

Expert Review

Few cars deliver this much driving joy for the money. The shifter is mechanical perfection, the helical LSD pulls you out of corners, and the steering is genuinely communicative. Daily drive it without complaint, take it to a track day Saturday — this is the everyman's enthusiast car.
