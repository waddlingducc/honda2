Overview: Honda HR-V

Honda's small, smart, attainable SUV.

The 2026 Honda HR-V is built on the Civic platform, which means it inherits the Civic's refined ride and excellent safety scores while offering a higher seating position and more cargo room. With 24.4 cu ft behind the rear seats and 55.1 cu ft with seats folded, the HR-V is more practical than its compact dimensions suggest. Available in FWD or AWD, it's the right choice for buyers who want a Honda SUV but don't need (or want to pay for) a CR-V.

How It Compares to Competitors

The HR-V competes with the Toyota Corolla Cross, Mazda CX-30, and Subaru Crosstrek. Honda offers the most refined ride and best safety scores. Subaru wins for off-road capability with standard AWD; Mazda for interior quality. The HR-V is the most balanced overall.

Expert Review

The HR-V is the right-sized Honda SUV for many buyers. It's not as cargo-capable as the CR-V, but it's $4,000 cheaper, easier to park, and has the same Honda virtues.
