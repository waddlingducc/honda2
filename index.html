Why Buy a Honda in 2026?

Legendary Reliability

Honda consistently ranks among the most reliable brands in J.D. Power, Consumer Reports, and iSeeCars studies. Many Hondas easily top 250,000 miles.

Every 2026 Honda includes the Honda Sensing safety suite as standard, with most models earning 5-star NHTSA ratings and IIHS Top Safety Pick+ awards.

Strong Resale Value

Honda's Civic, Accord, and CR-V regularly make the lists of vehicles with the best resale value, retaining 50%+ of their value after 5 years.

Hybrid & EV Leadership

From the all-new Civic Hybrid to the Honda 0 Series electric SUV and Saloon, Honda offers fuel-efficient and electric options across every segment.
